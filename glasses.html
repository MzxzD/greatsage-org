Janet's idea: Made in EU

We're aiming for J.A.N.E.T. Glasses — Janet-native hardware designed and built in Europe, so the same warm, offline plaza vibe can live on something you truly own. Materials, components, and assembly in the EU/EEA. Shorter supply chains, stronger privacy, European regulation.

The EU's "Made in Europe" (opens in new tab) rules require at least 50% value created in the EU for qualification. Phase 1 validates the model on Halo; Phase 4 delivers glasses that meet this standard.

Janet's vision: wearables that respect you, your data, and your region — co-op, not captive.
